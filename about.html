About The Entrust Initiative

Nurturing worship leaders and teams for transformative experiences.

Who We Are

Entrust is a dynamic initiative dedicated to nurturing and developing the next generation of worship leaders, instrumentalists, vocalists, and technical experts in sound and media.

We believe in the power of worship to transform lives and communities and we are committed to equipping individuals with the skills and knowledge they need to lead and serve effectively.

Our foundation is built upon fostering deeper intimacy with God, building strong community bonds, and pursuing excellence in every aspect of worship ministry.

Our Vision

Our vision is to see worship ministry teams equipped with the necessary skills, knowledge, and spiritual foundation to lead transformative worship experiences that draw believers into deeper intimacy with God and foster community among congregants. We envision worship that is characterized by excellence, authenticity, and unity, ultimately glorifying God and edifying His people.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide comprehensive training and support to worship ministry teams, encompassing worship leaders, instrumentalists, sound engineers, media volunteers, and backup vocalists. Through practical instruction, spiritual formation, and collective worship experiences, we aim to empower participants to serve effectively in their respective roles, leading with humility, authenticity, and a deep reverence for God.

Our Core Values

Excellence

We strive for excellence in all aspects of worship ministry, honoring God with the highest standards of musical proficiency, technical skill, and creative expression. This involves continuous learning, practice, and dedication to offering our best in service.

Spiritual Formation

We are committed to fostering spiritual growth and maturity among participants, emphasizing the importance of personal devotion, prayer, and biblical study in worship leadership. True worship flows from a heart connected to God.

Unity

We prioritize unity within worship ministry teams and the broader church community, recognizing that our collective worship is most powerful when we are united in purpose and spirit. We encourage collaboration, mutual support, and humility.
