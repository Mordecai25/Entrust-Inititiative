Our Training Programs

Comprehensive training for every role in the worship ministry.

Worship Leader Training

Develop essential leadership skills rooted in strong theological understanding and practical application.

Our training focuses on equipping worship leaders to guide congregations effectively, fostering an environment of genuine worship and connection with God. Key areas include:

  • Theological foundations of worship
  • Developing a worship set & song selection
  • Leading a worship team effectively (rehearsals, communication)
  • Platform presence and engaging the congregation
  • Spiritual formation for worship leaders
  • Practical workshops and mentoring sessions

Instrumentalists Training

Receive specialized instruction in musical proficiency, technical skills, and band dynamics.

This program is designed for musicians serving on worship teams, aiming to enhance their contribution through skill development and understanding of team collaboration. Training covers:

  • Advanced techniques for specific instruments (Guitar, Keys, Drums, Bass, etc.)
  • Music theory applied to worship music
  • Playing together as a cohesive unit (dynamics, timing, arrangement)
  • Understanding audio engineering basics relevant to musicians
  • Hands-on experience and mentorship opportunities
  • Improving sound quality and tone

Media & Sound Engineers Training

Gain expertise in the technical aspects of worship production, from audio reinforcement to visual storytelling.

Essential training for those supporting worship through technology. We cover best practices and practical skills in:

  • Live sound mixing and sound system operation
  • Microphone techniques and stage setup
  • Media production (video capture, basic editing)
  • Presentation software (ProPresenter, etc.) operation and design
  • Visual storytelling through video, graphics, and stage design
  • Instruction on capturing and conveying the worship experience
